  • Stocks: Momentum, Pairs, Mean Reversion, Sector Rotation
  • FX: Carry + Trend, Momentum, Volatility Breakout
  • Options: Deep Surrogates, TDGF, Vol Surface Arb
  • Cross-Asset: Kronos Forecasting, Sentiment

Ensemble Method

  • Bayesian weighting: 60% Sharpe-based + 40% regime-based
  • 4-state regime detector (calm/volatile x trending/choppy)
  • Walk-forward cross-validation with embargo gap
  • Correlation monitoring (>0.85 triggers 50% reduction)
