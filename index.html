Risk Management

5 automated safety guardrails, Redis-backed circuit breaker, persistent portfolio risk manager

Circuit Breaker

  • High Water Mark: 5.0% drawdown halts trading
  • Start of Day: 3.0% intraday loss halts session
  • Redis + PostgreSQL dual persistence
  • Fail-closed: Redis down = no trading

Safety Guardrails

  • Signal Variance Guard (std < 0.01 halts)
  • Leverage Gate (max 1.5x gross)
  • Execution Monitor (25% failure pauses)
  • Model Promotion Gate (Sharpe > 0.5)
  • Calibration Health Check at startup

Portfolio Risk Manager

  • VaR (99%) and CVaR (95%) monitoring
  • Correlation alerts (>0.85 = 50% cut)
  • Per-strategy kill switches
  • 30-day historical seeding at startup
  • Reports logged to PostgreSQL
